✂About us ✂


         Jôel Cheong Hair Studio is a professional hair studio founded by Joel Cheong in 2019. Designer Joel Cheong has more than 9 years of perm experience and has served as a perm tutor for many salons in Malaysia.

         "People First" is the founding philosophy of Jôel Cheong Hair Studio. The health of your hair is always our top priority. In order to prevent damage to your hair, each of our dye/perm services comes with customized repair work to restore its moisture as well as to ensure hair remains unscathed and unaffected by any hair products or chemical works.

         Whether you are pursuing a bold haircut, trendy look, retro elegance or still in the search for one that suits you best, we are here to break through the general public's definition of "beauty" with you and create a bespoke style for you.

         We are breaking the general stereotype of a hair salon, and we invite you to discover and experience how different we are from the norm.




         Jôel Cheong Hair Studio 创立于2019年，是一家专业的造型工作室，由我们的造型师Joel Cheong所创办。设计师Joel Cheong 不仅拥有9年多的烫发经验，更曾担任多家发廊的烫发导师。

         以人为核心，是 Jôel Cheong Hair Studio的创办理念。您的健康发质，是我们十分重视的事情。为了不让发质受损，我们所提供的每一套染/烫发服务都包括了秀发修护工作，让干枯毛躁的秀发恢复水润光泽，并且确保顾客的发质健康，不受任何化学处理及头发造型产品所影响。
